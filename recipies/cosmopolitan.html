Cosmopolitan Cocktail

Description

It's got a brilliant pink color and tangy, refreshing flavor: yes, it's a Cosmo! A Cosmopolitan cocktail, to be exact. This great cocktail is ready to spice up your next gathering, be it a dinner party or drinks on the patio with friends.

What's in a Cosmo? The classic version of this cocktail uses Citron vodka, cranberry juice (usually sweetened), Cointreau, and lime.

Ingredients

Serving Size: 1

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • 1/2 ounce Cointreau
  • 1 1/2 ounces cranberry juice
  • 1 ounce sweet and sour lime mix
  • Garnish: lime peel

Steps

  1. Add the vodka, Cointreau, lime juice, and cranberry juice cocktail into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with a lime peel.
