Pasta Sauce

Description

This butter and garlic cream sauce comes together in just minutes and is the perfect topper for pasta and ravioli!.

This garlic and butter cream sauce is restaurant worthy that is done in less than 10 minutes and has less than 5 ingredients!.

Ingredients

serving size: 4.

Prep Time: 5 min. Cook Time: 5 min

  • 1/3 Cup Butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons Minced Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Whole wheat white flour
  • 1 Cup Whole milk, warmed
  • pinch of dried oregano, basil and parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once it begins to melt, take it off the heat and swirl the pan to allow the butter to continue to melt. Once the butter is fully melted, add the garlic and cook for approximately 30 seconds to 1 minute, stirring.
  2. Add the flour and whisk for 1 minute to cook off any raw flour taste.
  3. Slowly, whisk in the milk, a little at a time.
  4. Add the spices and continue cooking over a very low heat for a few minutes until thick. Keep warm until ready to serve.
  5. Serve over your dish.
